Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

SlashCart ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website at slashcart.app and our application at slashcart-app.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email address — when you sign up for our waitlist.
  • Grocery list content — text or images you submit to get price comparisons. This is processed to identify pantry staples and find prices. We do not store your grocery lists permanently.
  • Usage data — general information about how you use the app (pages visited, searches performed). This is used to improve the product.
  • Cookies — we use cookies to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide price comparison results for your grocery list.
  • To send you product updates and launch announcements (waitlist only).
  • To improve our product and fix bugs.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Affiliate Links & Third-Party Services

SlashCart participates in affiliate programs with Amazon and Walmart. When you click "Buy" links on our site, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review:

  • Amazon Privacy Notice
  • Walmart Privacy Policy

We use ScraperAPI to retrieve publicly available product pricing information from retailer websites.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., Supabase for database, Vercel for hosting, Anthropic for AI processing).
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law.

5. Data Retention

Waitlist email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. Price cache data is automatically deleted after 24 hours. We do not retain your grocery list content after your session ends.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Unsubscribe from our waitlist at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@slashcart.app.

7. Children's Privacy

SlashCart is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
hello@slashcart.app
