How it works

Upload your list.
We do the rest.

01

Paste or snap your list

Type it out, paste it in, or upload a photo of your grocery list or store receipt. Any format works — AI handles the parsing.

02

We fetch real prices

SlashCart scrapes live prices from Amazon and Walmart — not estimates, not cached data from last month. What you see is what they're charging today.
